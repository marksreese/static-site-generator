# Brandy and Cognac Cocktails

## Originals

### China Fight

- Shake + fine strain
- Coupe
- 1.5 cognac
- 0.75 China China
- 0.25 St Germain
- Orange twist

### Newark (Meehan)

- Stir
- Coupe
- 2 apple brandy (Laird's)
- 1 sweet vermouth (Vya)
- 0.25 Fernet Branca
- 0.25 maraschino

### Banana Spider (Estopinal)

- Shake
- Coupe
- 1.5 pisco
- 0.75 lemon
- 0.5 banana (Giffard du Bresil)
- Barspoon simple
- 1 white
- Spray Angostura

### My Fair Lady (Whisler's)

## Classics

### Pisco Sour

- Coupe
- 2 pisco (La Diablada)
- 0.75 lime

- 1 white
- 2 Angostura

- Coupe
- 2 VSOP (Remy Martin or 1840)
- 0.75 lemon

- Barspoon demerara
