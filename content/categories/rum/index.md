# Rum and Cachaca Cocktails

## Classics

### Daiquiri

- Shake + fine strain
- Coupe
- 2 rum
- 0.75 lime
- 0.25 simple
- Lime wheel

### Mojito

- Shake + fine strain
- Collins
- Ice
- 2 gold rum (Cana Brava 3)
- 1 soda
- 0.5 simple
- 0.75 lime
- 10 mint leaves muddled
